Better Sonos Controller — Help

Touch-friendly controller for Sonos speakers on your local network. Status updates automatically every few seconds.

Installation (quick guide)

  1. One-line install (Mac/Linux Terminal):
    curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/rggnkmp/BetterSonosController/main/install.sh | bash
  2. Start the server:
    ./install.sh --start
    Or in one go: …/install.sh | bash -s -- --start
  3. Open in browser: http://127.0.0.1:8766/ (this computer) or http://YOUR-COMPUTER-IP:8766/ on your phone (same Wi‑Fi).
  4. Optional auto-start (Mac): ./install.sh --autostart

Speakers not found? Edit config/sonos-mobile.env and set SONOS_IP to your speaker addresses. Full instructions are in the project README.md.

Screen layout

Now playing
Shown at the top when any speaker is playing or paused. Displays album art, track, artist, transport controls, and volume (single speaker) or group members (group).
Other speakers
Grid of speakers that are not part of the active group. Use the grip handle to move them.
Error banner
Red box at the top when Sonos is unreachable or an action fails.
No playback
Placeholder when nothing is playing or paused on any speaker.

Transport & playback

  • Previous track — skips to the previous track in the queue.
  • Play — starts playback on the active speaker or group.
  • Pause — pauses the active speaker or group.
  • Next track — skips to the next track in the queue.

Timeline slider — drag to seek within the current track. Elapsed and total time are shown below.

Volume & mute

  • Unmuted — tap to mute this speaker.
  • Muted — tap to unmute.

Volume slider — adjusts volume for a single speaker or each group member individually (0–100%).

Grouping speakers

  • Drag handle (grip) — drag a speaker from “Other speakers” onto the now-playing area to join the group. Hold ~0.3 s on touch devices, then drag.
  • ★
    Coordinator — the group leader (shown with ★ next to the name). Playback and queue are controlled through the coordinator.
  • Leave group — removes this speaker from the group (red icon, member rows only).
  • Make coordinator — hand group leadership to this speaker (star icon, member rows only).
  • Dismiss group — keeps this speaker playing alone and removes all other members from the group (red icon, right side of member row).

Drop on “Other speakers” — dragging a speaker there removes it from its current group (unjoin).

Tips

  • Changes apply instantly; the UI may briefly show optimistic feedback before the server confirms.
  • Album art is loaded via a local proxy when available.
  • All speakers must be on the same network as this server.
  • Add this page to your home screen for quick access (Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen).
